ūrumadhyabhyā-nnamaḥ ॥

hṛdayāya namaḥ ॥

mukhāya namaḥ ।

haṃsa gāyatrī

Asya śrī hansagāyatrīstōtra mahāmantrasya। ātmā r̥ṣiḥ। paramā̎tmā dēvatā। avyaktagāyatrī-chandaḥ। hansagāyatrījapē viniyōgaḥ॥

hansāṁ aṅguṣṭhābhyāṁ namaḥ ॥ hansīṁ tarjanībhyāṁ namaḥ hansūṁ madhyamābhyāṁ namaḥ hansaiṁ anāmikābhyāṁ namaḥ hansauṁ kaniṣṭhikābhyāṁ namaḥ hansaḥ karatala karapr̥ṣṭhābhyāṁ namaḥ hansāṁ hr̥dayāya namaḥ

Hansīṁ śirasē svāhā
hansūṁ śikhāyai vaṣaṭ
hansaiṁ kavacāya huṁ
hansaunnētratrayāya vauṣaṭ
hansaḥ astrāya phaṭ
bhūrbhuvas'suvarōmiti digbandhaḥ॥
