Edugurralapally Project

Eduguralapally is a very remote tribal village, where people did not have electricity and water facility. It was very tough for any official to reach at the hilltop, and help them with basic needs.
Rudram school raised funds to provided them the financial aid needed along with medicines and daily foods and vegetables and sanitation. Rudram school colloborated with Matrubhoomi to help the village. Guru garu taught them Shri Rudram and gave them a better life, with proper electricity, water, huts, and more.
