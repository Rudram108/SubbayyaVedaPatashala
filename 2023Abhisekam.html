Shata Rudra Abhisekam 2022

Guru garu performs Rudra Abhisekam at the devotee homes in places like San Antonio , Bornea , Austin. Guru garu brings all the items needed for abhisekam and expects only pure love and devotion towards Lord Shiva from the hosts. This event is free of cost and any donations raised will be given to Veda Schools in India.
