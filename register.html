Why register with Rudram School


Registration is very important. Learning vedas without a guru is always fruitless. Registration with us will give you divine knoweldge of veda mantras through a guru.
Once you register with us, you will join weekend zoom classes with Subbaya Shastry Garu. Class goes on for a duration of 1 hour. In this hour, registered user, will learn the proper pronunciation of veda mantras and may also get assessed by guru garu directly.
The class might extend beyond an hour if the learner wishes to continue. Learning veda mantras is a lifetime achievment so to provide additional assistance for perfection, every registered user may join a smaller practice group. These groups help practice over the week days and clear any doubts that could not be answered over weekends. Every practice group has a mentor or leader, who help the registered user,correct the mistakes and enjoy the mantra vibrations. At times, Guru garu also joins the groups to help the learning process. During weekend classes, Guru garu patiently hears to the recitations for every registered user and corrects their mistakes. This process of correcting mistakes within the group and then with guru garu , continues until a flawless chanting is achieved.

What's the fee


All the fee needs to be paid in terms of time and dedication. This school is established to spread the vedic knowledge and to spread the vibrations of vedic mantra across the cosmos. Earning materialistic is not the motive of this school.
