Subbayya Veda Patashala


Rudram school under the guidance of Shri Subbaya Shastry garu has worked on the below projects. Shastry garu was involved in many other social activities which he never discusses with students. Below are just a few as far as the senior students memory extends.
Shri Rudra Homam at Chidambaram
Shri Rudra Homam at Chidambaram
Subbaya Shastry garu and Rudram school participated in Chidambaram Sri Nataraja Temple Shri Rudra Homam and Anna Prasadam . This served 1 Lac people in 23 districts.
Shatha SriRudraParayana
Shatha SriRudraParayana
Guru garu along with the Rudram School selected team memebers, visited more than 108 homes and performed Shri Rudra abhisekam at their homes.
Eduguralapally Project
Eduguralapally Tribal Village
Rudram school collaborated with Matrubhoomi team and gave financial aid needed to build a better living are with pure water and plantation.
Vedic Schools Project
Vedic Schools Project
Rudram school contributes to promotion of vedic knowledge in future generation by adopting vedic students.
