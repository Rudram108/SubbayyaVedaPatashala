॥ ōṃ namah shivaya ॥ athah mahanyasa prayogaha॥ ōṃ namō bhagavatē̍ rudrā̠ya ॥


ōṃ athātaḥ pañchāṅgarudrāṇām॥


ō-mbhūrbhuva̠ssuva̍ḥ ॥


ō-nnam nama̍stē rudra ma̠nyava̍ u̠tōta̠ iṣa̍vē̠ nama̍ḥ ।
nama̍stē astu̠ dhanva̍nē bā̠hubhyā̍mu̠ta tē̠ nama̍ḥ ॥
ō-m kam kham gam gham ngam ।
ō-nnamō bhagavatē̍ rudrā̠ya ॥ nam ō-m purvanga rudraya namah ॥


mahādēva-mmahātmāna-mmahāpātakanāśanam । mahāpāpaharaṃ vandē makārāya namō namaḥ ॥

ō-mbhūrbhuva̠ssuva̍ḥ ॥


ō-m mam nidha̍napatayē̠ namaḥ । nidhanapatāntikāya̠ namaḥ ।
ūrdhvāya̠ namaḥ । ūrdhvaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।

hiraṇyāya̠ namaḥ । hiraṇyaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।
suvarṇāya̠ namaḥ । suvarṇaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।

divyāya̠ namaḥ । divyaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।
bhavāya̠ḥ namaḥ । bhavaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।

śarvāya̠ namaḥ । śarvaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।
śivāya̠ namaḥ । śivaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।

jvalāya̠ namaḥ । jvalaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।
ātmāya̠ namaḥ । ātmaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।
paramāya̠ namaḥ । paramaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।

ētatsōmasya̍ sūrya̠sya sarvaliṅgagg̍ sthāpa̠ya̠ti̠ pāṇimantra-mpavi̠tram ॥


ō-m cham Cham jam jham nam । ō-nnamō bhagavatē̍ rudrā̠ya ॥ mam ō-m ॥ dakṣiṇāṅga rudrāya̠ namaḥ ॥


śivaṃ śānta-ñjagannāthaṃ lōkānugrahakāraṇam । śivamēka-mparaṃ vandē śikārāya namō namaḥ ॥ ō-mbhūrbhuva̠ssuva̠ḥ ॥


ōṃ śim ॥ apai̍tumṛ̠tyuramṛta̍-nna̠ āga̍n vaivasva̠tō nō̠ a̍bhaya-ṅkṛṇōtu ।

pa̠rṇaṃ vana̠spatērivā̠bhinaśśīyatāgṃ ra̠yissacha̍tā-nna̠śśachī̠pati̍ḥ ॥


ōṃ Tam Tham Dam Dham Nam ।ō-nnamō bhagavatē̍ rudrā̠ya ॥ śim ōṃ ॥ paśchimāṅga rudrāya̠ namaḥ ॥


vāhanaṃ vṛṣabhō yasya vāsukī kaṇṭhabhūṣaṇam । vāmē śaktidharaṃ vandē vakārāya namō namaḥ ॥ ō-mbhūrbhuva̠ssuva̠ḥ ॥


ōṃ vām ॥ prāṇānā-ṅgranthirasi rudrō mā̍ viśā̠ntakaḥ । tēnānnēnā̎pyāya̠sva ॥


ōṃ tam tham dam dham nam । ō-nnamō bhagavatē̍ rudrā̠ya ॥ vām ōṃ ॥ uttarāṅga rudrāya̠ namaḥ ॥


yatra kutra sthita-ndēvaṃ sarvavyāpinamīśvaram । yalliṅga-mpūjayēnnityaṃ yakārāya namō namaḥ ॥
ō-mbhūrbhuva̠ssuva̠ḥ ॥


ōṃ yam ॥ yō ru̠drō a̠gnau yō a̠psu ya ōṣa̍dhīṣu̠ yō ru̠drō viśvā̠ bhuva̍nā vi̠vēśa̠ tasmai̍ ru̠drāya̠ namō̍ astu ॥


ōṃ pam pham bam bham mam । ō-nnamō bhagavatē̍ rudrā̠ya ॥ yam ōṃ ॥ ūrdhvāṅga rudrāya̠ namaḥ ॥


tatpuru̠ṣāya vi̠dmahē̍ mahādē̠vāya̍ dhīmahi । tannō̍ rudraḥ prachōdayā̎t ॥


saṃvartāgni taṭitpradīpta kanaka prasparthi tējōmayam । gambhīradhvani sāmavēdajanaka-ntāmrādharaṃ sundaram । ardhēndudyuti lōlapiṅgaḻa jaṭābhāraprabaddhōragam । vandē siddha surāsurēndranamita-mpūrva-mmukhaṃ śūlinaḥ ॥
