॥ ōṃ namaḥ Shivaya ॥
॥ athā̍ mahanyasa prayogaha ॥
॥ ō-nnamō bhagavatē̍ rudrā̠ya ॥


॥ athātaḥ pañchāṅga rudrāṇām ॥


॥ ō-m bhūrbhuva̠ssuva̍ḥ ॥


ōṃ nam nama̍stē rudra ma̠nyava̍ u̠tōta̠ iṣa̍vē̠ nama̍ḥ ।
nama̍stē astu̠ dhanva̍nē bā̠hubhyā̍mu̠ta tē̠ nama̍ḥ ॥।
ōṃ kaṁ khaṁ gaṁ ghaṁ ṅaṁ ।
ō-nnamō bhagavatē̍ rudrā̠ya । nam ōṃ। pūrvāṅga rudrāya̠ namaḥ॥


mahādēva-mmahātmāna-mmahāpātakanāśanam ।
mahāpāpaharaṃ vandē makārāya namō namaḥ ॥


॥ ō-m bhūrbhuva̠ssuva̍ḥ ॥


mam ōṃ nidha̍napatayē̠ namaḥ । nidhanapatāntikāya̠ namaḥ ।
ūrdhvāya̠ namaḥ । ūrdhvaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।

hiraṇyāya̠ namaḥ । hiraṇyaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।
suvarṇāya̠ namaḥ । suvarṇaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।

divyāya̠ namaḥ । divyaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।
bhavāya̠ḥ namaḥ । bhavaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।

śarvāya̠ namaḥ । śarvaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।
śivāya̠ namaḥ । śivaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।

jvalāya̠ namaḥ । jvalaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।
ātmāya̠ namaḥ । ātmaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।
paramāya̠ namaḥ । paramaliṅgāya̠ namaḥ ।


ētatsōmasya̍ sūrya̠sya sarvaliṅgagg̍ sthāpa̠ya̠ti̠ pāṇimantra-mpavi̠tram ॥


ōṃ caṁ chaṁ jaṁ jhaṁ ñaṁ । ō-nnamō bhagavatē̍ rudrā̠ya ।
mam ōṃ dakṣiṇāṅga rudrāya̠ namaḥ ॥


śivaṃ śānta-ñjagannāthaṃ lōkānugrahakāraṇam ।śivamēka-mparaṃ vandē śikārāya namō namaḥ ॥
ō-mbhūrbhuva̠ssuva̠ḥ ॥


ōṃ śim apai̍tumṛ̠tyuramṛta̍-nna̠ āga̍n vaivasva̠tō nō̠ a̍bhaya-ṅkṛṇōtu ।

pa̠rṇaṃ vana̠spatērivā̠bhinaśśīyatāgṃ ra̠yissacha̍tā-nna̠śśachī̠pati̍ḥ ॥


ōṃ ṭaṁ ṭhaṁ ḍaṁ ḍhaṁ ṇaṁ । ō-nnamō bhagavatē̍ rudrā̠ya।śim ōṃ paśchimāṅga rudrāya̠ namaḥ॥


vāhanaṃ vṛṣabhō yasya vāsukī kaṇṭhabhūṣaṇam ।vāmē śaktidharaṃ vandē vakārāya namō namaḥ ॥
ō-mbhūrbhuva̠ssuva̠ḥ ॥


ōṃ vām prāṇānā-ṅgranthirasi rudrō mā̍ viśā̠ntakaḥ। tēnānnēnā̎pyāya̠sva ॥


ōṃ taṁ thaṁ daṁ dhaṁ naṁ । ō-nnamō bhagavatē̍ rudrā̠ya ।
vām ōṃ uttarāṅga rudrāya̠ namaḥ॥


yatra kutra sthita-ndēvaṃ sarvavyāpinamīśvaram। yalliṅga-mpūjayēnnityaṃ yakārāya namō namaḥ ॥॥
ō-mbhūrbhuva̠ssuva̠ḥ ॥


ōṃ yam yō ru̠drō a̠gnau yō a̠psu ya ōṣa̍dhīṣu̠ yō ru̠drō viśvā̠ bhuva̍nā vi̠vēśa̠ tasmai̍ ru̠drāya̠ namō̍ astu ॥


ōṃ paṁ phaṁ baṁ bhaṁ maṁ ।ō-nnamō bhagavatē̍ rudrā̠ya ।
yam ōṃ ūrdhvāṅga rudrāya̠ namaḥ ॥
